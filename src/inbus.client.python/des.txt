MessageReceiver
---------------
Responsibilities:
Waits for raw Inbus formatted messages
Converts them into a Message (using MessageTranslator)
Sends this Message to its Listeners

Collaborator: MessageTranslator, to translate the message

MessageTranslator
-----------------
Translates raw Inbus messages to either a Subscribe, Unsubscribe or Publish
method, and invokes those method on its InbusMethodObserver

Registry isA MesssageListener
--------
Manages a list of subscribers.
- Subscribe: add a subscriber
- Unsubscribe: remove a subscriber
- Subscribers: returns a list of subscribers 

Broadcaster isA MesssageListener
-----------
